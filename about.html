What a beautiful city

A couple of months ago I had to take a descición. I had to choose between moving in with my girlfriend to a beautiful city full of culture, history and amazing people or stay in the same place where I had lived my childhood and pick up the pieces of my heart (I can't live without her). So it was easy for me to take a descición.

There is always a beautiful place that you still don't know

Usually the stories have a beginning, then comes a moment where everything is complicated but then using the virtues of main character He overcome all obstacles and move to the happy ending.

Our story is just beginning, I would say don't even have written the foreword but if I had to put a title of the first chapter would choose "What a beautiful city".

But there never seems to be enough time to do the things you want to do, once you find them
